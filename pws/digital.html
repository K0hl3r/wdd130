DIGITAL DRAWINGS

This is the Digital Drawings page. The future is now, the artist may be more comfortable with his traditional art but he is no stranger to his more digital side. The programs the artist is familiar with are those in the Adobe Creative Suite, such as Photoshop and Illustrator. The artist can also adapt to different programs if needed. The drawings on this page will consist half of the artist's linework and the other half will add a splash of color. The artist's linework will show his capability to create clean lines with nothing but a screen and a digital pen. Once he adds color, the art will stand out ten times better.
