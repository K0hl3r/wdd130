WELCOME

Hello and welcome to the portfolio of Rebel Arts. Here you will find a variety of drawings, from Traditional Drawings to Digital Drawings. A little bit about the artist, Kohler Pratt is 23 years old and is a self-taught artist who is set on becoming a comic book illustrator. He is currently attending BYU Idaho but that dosent mean he can't find time to do the thing he loves most, drawing. The medium Kohler prefers the most is pencil and ink, He would make an excellent penciller or inker. He is also very knowlegable in digital mediums, such as those found with the adobe creative suite. Kohler loves the world of comics and want to be a bigger part of it.
