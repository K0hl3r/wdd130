Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Dry Oar, we are excited to bring the wonderful word of rafting to everyone. Our services provide an all inclusive multi-day expirence down the rivers of Colorado and we want to bring these services to a larger audience. Our website will provide contact info, our locations, what you can expect from our adventures, ect. You can expect to see images of different rivers and the nature surrounds them, guest expirences, and a list of river guides.

Audience

Our target audience are going to be those who seek high adventure. They want to see nature, expirence thrills, and to live life to the fullest. They can be anyone from college students to boy scouts. now a days people tend to live, work, and play inside their own house, our audience will want an escape from this. Our audience will be able to access our website from desktop, mobile device or personal computer.
